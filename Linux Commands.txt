Linux Terminal Commands:

pwd (print work director?): Show us our main section that we are working with in our main directory on the Linux terminal.
cat: Cammand used to read our files in linux terminal.
ls: List all the files that are inside ou main directory.
ls -l: Give us more details about the list.
ls -a: Show us the list and also show us hidden files.
ls -la: Show us a list that combine the commands ls -l with ls -a.
clear: clear the screen on the terminal.
